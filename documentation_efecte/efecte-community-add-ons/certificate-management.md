    Certificate management
  
  
   Here you can find a solution for managing your certificate data with ESM and ESS.  
 You can store the data of your certificates in CMDB and publish the ordering of certificates in Self-service portal.  
 Certificate requests are handled with your existing Service requests in ESM.  
 The certificate management includes also the email notifications and archiving of expiring certificates.
