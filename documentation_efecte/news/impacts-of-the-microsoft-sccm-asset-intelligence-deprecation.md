 As announced by Microsoft, the asset intelligence feature of SCCM is deprecated. Based on our analysis, the deprecation does not affect the Efecte Service Management tool's SCCM connectors (SCCMIntegrationTask and SCCMUsageTask). The SCCM Asset Intelligence Deprecation has no impact on hardware inventory, custom hardware inventory, basic software inventory, and other essential features of the asset intelligence component.   
 The following SCCM functionality is deprecated according to Microsoft:  
 1) Asset intelligence catalog  
 2) Asset intelligence synchronization point  
 3) Microsoft Volume License import and reconciliation  
 All these functionalities are directly linked with the System Center Cloud solution, and only system center online cloud synchronization-related components of Asset Intelligence will be deprecated/removed by Microsoft.  
 Please refer to Microsoft's documentation for further information on the deprecation.
