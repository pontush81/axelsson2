Efecte Admin Tips - More alternatives for learning - Admin training goes digital

      
    

        updated 1 mth agoWed, December 10, 2025 at 11:33 AM GMT+1
  
          

        
    
Have you already noticed that we offer our main admin trainings in digital format also? 
Face-to-face training has been – and we believe it will continue to be – the most wanted form of training for administrators. However, we acknowledge the diverse needs that have arisen during the past years in many organizations and want to offer more alternatives and flexibility for learning.
By purchasing the digital training service, you can familiarize yourself with Efecte’s solutions with video materials whenever it suits you and wherever you are. After learning with the training video, you can then test your knowledge by taking the Efecte certification exams. What is more – you can revisit the videos and brush up on your skills, as the content is available for you throughout the subscription.
The first available courses are "Administering Service Management Tool" and "Administering Efecte Self-Service Portal".
More detailed info and request for service here.
We also continue to create short how-to videos on Efecte solutions. The how-to videos showcase both new and existing features that simplify administrators' daily work. The videos are published regularly in the Tips for Administrators area here in the Efecte community and are free of charge.

Thanks for reading, and as always, don't hesitate to contact us in case you have any questions!
- Team Efecte
Haven't attended admin training yet or looking for a refresh? View upcoming dates and enroll to one of our public trainings here or send us email.
