  ContentsWhat’s new & exciting?On ESM/ITSM domain:On IGA domain:What’s new & exciting?
On ESM/ITSM domain:

 "AI Agent for Ticket Preparation" (summaries, categorization, resolutions, auto-assignment, KB creation)
 Readiness for “AI Agent for Intelligent Response”
 Note: This requires separate setup, since the capability is based on the knowledge provided by Knowledge Discovery.
 Renewed use cases for AI Writing assistance and Configurable AI use cases (in the Actions panel) with ready-made prompts.
 Updated templates, improved translations, and several quality-of-life enhancements
 Integrated Raynet solution, including the connector, configuration, and demo data, all available out-of-the-box.

On IGA domain:

 New risk calculation model, including risk levels for identities and entitlements.
 Improved entitlement descriptions, now supporting long text fields and updated connector mappings for AD, Azure and LDAP.
 Smarter workflows, including fixes for manager/subordinate creation timing, approval logic, and account activation timing.
 Better handling for employment changes, such as automatic OU move checks and clearing temporary contract end dates.
 Enhanced Work Period logic, including improvements in primary WP calculation.
 Improved access rights request process, now allowing entitlements to be requested both independently and as part of business roles.
 AI-powered entitlement data card creation, with new prompts for generating friendly names and descriptions automatically.
