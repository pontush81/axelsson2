    Efecte Baseline Release Notes 2022.2
  
  
   Efecte Baseline 2022.2 concentrates on general configuration improvements for both, ITSM and IGA solutions. Some of the highlights are:  
 
 AD Group -template is removed in this baseline version and only IGA Entitlement -template is needed in configuration. 
 The provisioning process is streamlined with configuration on IGA Account and Person -templates. 
 Teams Bot -attributes included.
